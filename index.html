Introduction
Empire Asia Holding Co., Ltd. (“EAH”) is a leading financial advisory and private investment company established in the Year 2000 with business interests and active projects in several business sectors and spanning the Asia Pacific and the Middle East. The company operates on two separate but interrelated levels. On one level, it provides strategic insight, financial engineering and corporate advice to its client companies as a specialist consulting company. On the other level, it actively participates in the investment and operations of the business units that it helps to create, adding tangible inputs to the management to help increase revenues, profitability and shareholder value. EAH has also created several subsidiary companies in selected jurisdictions around the world and with key partners and affiliates to further optimize its business objectives. With more than 40 years of business experience gathered from around the world, the principals of EAH, led by founding partner, Npairoj Piempongsant, have distinguished themselves in the fields of privatized infrastructure, industrial & commercial real estate development, conventional & alternative energy, telecommunications, sports management & promotion (primarily UK professional football), and lastly, general trade & investment. EAH’s principal offices are located in Bangkok, Thailand.

Current Focus - Privatized Infrastructure & Energy
 Today, EAH’s primary strength comes from its strategic focus and expertise in the integrated infrastructure and regional economic development sectors (smart cities, airports, warehouse & logistics parks, fintech, conventional & alternative energy and cross-border economic zones) ; coupled with its exceptional global network of high-net-worth investors, private companies, financial institutions and government agencies that it collaborates with throughout ASEAN and in strategic locations globally. This focus has given rise to the firm’s outstanding initiatives and active participation in several high-profile projects in the infrastructure sector especially in Thailand’s Eastern Seaboard (ESB) with the Thai Government’s inauguration in March 2018 of the Eastern Economic Corridor of Innovation (“EECi”) with its current PPP Tenders for the privatization of the U-Tapao International Airport Project, the High-Speed Train connecting Bangkok’s airports to U-Tapao, the development of a new Airport City “Aerotropolis” and the redevelopment of Ban Chang as a Smart City.

Active projects also include the development of raw and potable water supply for Ban Chang and Map Ta Phut Industrial Estate and various mixed-use real estate projects also located in Ban Chang. By employing a “total business approach and win-win execution principle” to its carefully selected projects and investments, coupled with its ability to attract exceptional local partners for all of its projects, EAH seeks always to achieve the vitally important first-in advantage in emerging markets, speedy & highest returns on investment and rapid overall value appreciation for its clients and investment partners

In addition to Privatized Infrastructure, EAH is also actively engaged in the Global Energy Sector today, primarily in the development of world-wide markets and in the investment in the required facilities, especially for LNG trading, as well as crude oil and other petroleum products. This new division of EAH was established in 2019 to serve as an effective and focused platform to not only pursue and execute selected projects in the oil, gas and energy sectors, but also to harness the vast experience and talent of some of the industry’s most influential players, including the Group’s Advisory Committee Chairman, Dr. Tongchat Hongladaromp, who for years was the driving force behind Thailand’s PTT as this leading Thai energy giant evolved and grew rapidly to become one of Asia’s and the world’s leading diversified energy companies. Aside from its diverse experience and reputation, the main strength of EAH’s Energy Division today is also derived from the group’s continually expanding network of client companies, strategic partners and global business leaders, especially focusing on the GCC, Israel, Russia and China, where inter-regional trade and investment opportunities are most promising.
